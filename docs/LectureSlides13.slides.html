3 basic systems you can investigate¶

  • 1 proton + 2 electrons orbiting in the same rotational directon: Tricky to find interesting things to investigate - very sensitive to small asymmetries.
  • 1 proton + 2 electrons orbiting in the opposite rotational directon: Creates lots of different types of "orbits"
  • 2 protons + 1 electron: Largest number of different things to vary and still have stable orbits.

You define stability¶

  • It doesn't have to be identical repeating orbits. Many shapes precess
  • It can be interesting to develelop indices or quality factors that characterize stability
